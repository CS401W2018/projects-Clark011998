Why I play video games?

The reason why I play video games is to have fun, and I find it as my stress reliever. It is also a bonding between me and my friends when we meet online and play.

NBA2k24

Official poster of NBA2k24 Kobe Bryant edition. NBA2k24 is an online game of basketball that makes you able to control a specific team you want.

VALORANT

Valorant is a first person shooter game that was published by Riot Games. it is an objective-based game.

Rainbow Six Siege

Rainbow Six Siege is game made by the company called Ubisoft. It is also a first person shooter tactical game. Each player has a role of either an attacker or defender.

OVERWATCH

Overwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter game that was created by the company called Blizzard Entertainment. Two teams, with six players each, compete.

LEFT4DEAD

Left4Dead game is a story about four survivors that must fight off infected humans while trying to make their way to a safe house or rescue vehicle. It is also a first-person shooter game which each of the player takes control of one of the four survivors inside the game.

League of Legends: Wildrift

League of Legends: Wildrift is a multiplayer online battle arena mobile game developed and published by Riot Games. It is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other's base.
