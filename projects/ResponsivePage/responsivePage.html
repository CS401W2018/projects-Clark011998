Here is the member of the family

Odin, the All-Father

Odin, the All-Father is the King of Asgard, and ruler of the nine realm. He is the God of Wisdom and War. He has won many battles alongside with his soldiers. He is left with one eye indicating the sacrifices he made for wisdom and leadership during wars. He has three children which are Thor, Loki, and Hela.

Frigga Borson

Frigga is the wife of Odin. Frigga is the Queen of Asgard. She is a sorceress, highly skilled in Asgardian magic and was responsible for teaching Loki much of what he knows now about illusions and magic. She is a loving mother, showing a deep bond to Thor and Loki, despite Loki being adopted. She tries to maintain peace between them.

Thor Odinson

Thor is the God of Thunder, who also wields the Mjolnir, a magical hammer that grants him control over thunder and lightning. Thor is a biological son of Odin, the rightful heir to the throne of Asgard. Thor matures significantly throughout the MCU, from an arrogant warrior, to a self-aware hero who accepts responsibility. Later on in the story, Mjolnir got destroyed, and was replaced by a weapon called Stormbreaker, an axe that is also a very powerful weapon.

Loki Laufeyson

Loki is actually adopted from the frost giants by Odin after the war with the frost giants. Loki is known to be the God of Mischief, known for his trickery and deception, he is often waver between villain and anti-hero in the MCU. He has a deep resentment for Odin due to feelings of rejection and inadequacy. But later on, Loki showed his redemption after he saved the people of Asgard from Thanos, and also fixing the multiverse.

Hela Odinsdottir

Hela is the firstborn daughter of Odin. She is the Goddess of Death. Before she was imprisoned, Hela helped Odin conquer many realms and was his enforcer in battle while fighting side by side. After she became too power-hungry, Odin banished Hela, imprisoning her for millenia, but was later on freed after Odin's death. Later on, Thor and Loki faced her as she wants to kill a lot of people and gain more power. The story goes on as the brothers try to stop their evil sister.
