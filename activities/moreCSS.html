What is the margin, border, and padding?

  • Margin - Clears an area outside the border, and it is transparent
  • Border - It goes around the padding and content.
  • Padding - Clears an area around the content. It is also transparent.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

We can use the pixels, points, centimeters, millimeters, and inches. We can also use percentage, and other things that are inside the system.

When would you use a figure tag?

When you want to insert and image, and write a figure caption that will be placed right before the image
